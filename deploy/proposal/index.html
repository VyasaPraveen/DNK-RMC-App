Understanding Your Requirement

Thank you for sharing your sample Tax Invoice and business requirement. Based on our review, DNK Power Conmix currently prepares Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) tax invoices manually — re-typing customer name, site address, GST details, vehicle number, concrete grade, quantity and GST calculations for every dispatch. This is time consuming and prone to manual errors.

"I want a software that can automatically generate bills like the attached, without manually typing the customer name, vehicle number, grade and quantity every time — with automatic GST calculation."

We propose a dedicated RMC Billing & Dispatch Management Software built around master data (customers, sites, vehicles, grades, rates), so that daily billing becomes a simple selection-based entry — reducing a 5–10 minute manual bill to under a minute, with zero calculation errors and a professional GST-compliant invoice every time.

Current Challenges

  • Manual retyping of customer & site details for every invoice
  • Manual GST & total amount calculation – risk of errors
  • No central record of past bills, hard to search old invoices
  • No visibility of outstanding customer payments
  • No backup – risk of losing billing history

What This Software Solves

  • One-time master setup of customers, vehicles, grades & rates
  • Auto GST calculation as per invoice format shown
  • Instant search of any past bill / customer / date
  • Real-time outstanding & payment tracking
  • Secure login with automatic data backup

Proposed Solution — Core Modules

Every package below is built on these foundation modules, so that a bill can be generated in a few clicks — no repeated typing of customer, vehicle or grade details.

  • Customer Master – select customer from dropdown instead of typing name, address & GSTIN every time
  • Site / Project Master – store multiple delivery sites per customer
  • Vehicle & Driver Master – save transit mixer numbers & drivers, select in one click
  • Concrete Grade Master – predefined grades (M15, M20, M20S, M25, M30 etc.) with HSN/SAC code
  • Rate Master – customer-wise & grade-wise rates, auto-fetched on bill entry
  • Daily Dispatch Entry – one screen to log each day's dispatch (customer, site, vehicle, grade, quantity)
  • Automatic GST Calculation – CGST/SGST or IGST auto-applied exactly as in your invoice format
  • Invoice Generation (PDF) – ready-to-print / WhatsApp-ready GST tax invoice, matching your current format
